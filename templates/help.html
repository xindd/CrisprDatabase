Data sources

With SRGIdb, we aim to capture the suppressors of RNA-guided antiviral defense systems in Prokaryotes and Eukaryotes, by providing:

381 VSRs obtained from 161 publications or ViralZone database (annotated in UniProtKB/Swiss-Prot):

331 protein sequences with different accession numbers;

>50 protein families including P0, P1, P19, P22, P3, P0, CP, B2, 2b, NSs, NS3, HC-Pro, P2, P126, P38, P37, P15, TGBp1(P25), Pns10, Pns11, Pns12, VP35, VP3, VPg, RNase III, Beta C1 etc.

30 verified anti-CRISPRs (ACRs) and 446 homologs reported in 7 publications

476 protein sequences with different accession numbers;

23 families including AcrF1-10, AcrE1-4, AcrIIA1-5, AcrIIC1-3, AcrID1

Service provided:

Browse page is used for easily browsing the detail information for ACRs and VSRs.

Search page is used for searching the records in our database using keywords or protein sequence.

Visualize page is used for summarizing the PDB structures and mechanism illustrations of the ACRs and VSRs.

Analyze page is used to analyze the genomic neighborhood of anti-CRISPR candidates. Analyze Download page is used to download the datasets underlying our database.
