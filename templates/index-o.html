SRGIdb:

An online database of suppressors of CRISPR-Cas and RNAi mediated defense systems for easy browsing of published anti-CRISPRs (Acrs) and viral suppressors of RNA silencing (VSRs).

Viral suppressors of RNA silencing (VSR):

To counteract host RNAi antiviral defense, viruses from almost all plant virus genera (and also from some insect and mammal viruses) encode viral suppressors of RNA silencing (VSRs) which inhibit key steps of cellular RNAi system.

Anti-CRISPRs (ACR):

Since CRISPR-Cas systems provide a potent defense mechanism against phage infection. On the contrary, phages possess anti-CRISPR proteins to inhibit these systems.

Overview and data sources

With SRGIdb, we aim to capture the suppressors of RNA-guided antiviral defense systems in Prokaryotes and Eukaryotes, by providing:

  • 381 VSRs obtained from 161 publications and ViralZone database:

    • 172 are collected from publications.
    • 269 are covered by ViralZone database.

  • 30 validated anti-CRISPRs obtained from 8 publications and 2751 homologs predicted using the blastp tool

    • 30 validated anti-CRISPRs are obtained from 8 publications.
    • 383 homologs of the 2751 are covered by 8 publications.
  • ** PDB structures
  • ** interactors of VSRs and Acrs
  • Explanation of the mechanism of action for 248 VSRs and 14 Acrs.
